About Game

Owl Brawl is an ambitious project designed as a fighting and party game, featuring a range of exciting game modes in its future plans. In its current state, the game offers a thrilling Versus Mode where players engage in fierce competition by attempting to push their opponents off the stage. With its whimsical physics and charming graphics, Owl Brawl emphasizes the importance of quick reflexes and strategic maneuvers. By default, players take control of an endearing owl character, with plans to introduce additional skins for further customization.


Technical Implementations

  • Custom Physics: Engineered a bespoke physics system, elevating the game's competitive environment and introducing engaging mechanics. The result is a blend of fun and skill-based gameplay, coupled with intuitive player movement.

  • Character Customization: Introduced a user-friendly customization feature, allowing players to personalize their character by altering their name and colors.

  • Versatile Input System: Implemented a versatile input system, accommodating both keyboard and controller inputs to ensure accessibility for all players.

  • VFX Integration: Enhanced the game's visual appeal by incorporating dazzling visual effects (VFX), with stars making a captivating appearance each time players clash with their opponents.

  • Artistic Creation: I meticulously crafted and implemented all artistic elements within the game, contributing to its unique charm.


Key Game Features

    Local Multiplayer:

    Dive into exhilarating local multiplayer sessions, pitting players against each other as they vie to outmaneuver their opponents and achieve victory by propelling them out of the stage.

    AI Opponents:

    Engage in battles against AI opponents, providing a solo gaming experience for those seeking a challenging encounter.

    Character Customization:

    Exercise creative freedom by customizing your character's name and colors, allowing for a touch of personalization in the midst of thrilling battles.
