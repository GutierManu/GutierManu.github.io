About Game

A project focused on demonstrating some of the capabilities of AR development in Unity. The app uses the camera to detect flat surfaces in the real world. Once these flat surfaces are detected, the user can instantiate a portal with 3 different environments (for this project, house areas are featured). The portal works both ways, the user is able to enter the digital environment by the front or the back side, and equally exit by any of these sides. The user is also able to exit (destroy) the environment by tapping on the pertinent button, allowing them to place the AR Portal in a different position.


Technical Implementation

  • Cross-Portal Shader: Innovatively crafted a specialized shader enabling seamless cross-portal interactions, a key feature of the app.

  • AR integration: Implemented surface detection and the portal placement/removal behavior. This infrastructure allows for effortless scalability, allowing for the replacement or addition of various environments.

  • UI Design and Integration: Designed and seamlessly integrated the user interface, offering an intuitive and user-friendly experience.

  • Language Localization: Implemented language localization, offering support in English, Spanish, and French, enabling users to switch languages effortlessly via the language button.


Key Game Features

    Explore the AR World:

    Embark on a captivating journey through different areas of a house by seamlessly crossing the AR portal. This feature opens up countless possibilities for advergames and a wide range of immersive experiences.

    Convenient Portal Placement:

    Enjoy the freedom to position the portal in the real world, allowing you to explore the other side in open areas that suit your preference. Change the portal's position at any time to enhance your exploration.

    Language Support:

    Customize your AR experience by choosing your preferred language—English, Spanish, or French—with a simple tap on the language button.
