About Game

Created for the Opera GX Game Jam held in Game Jolt in August 2021, this game was designed around the theme "UFOs and the Internet" and developed within a two-week time frame. In this game, players embark on a solo adventure or join forces in multiplayer mode, facing the formidable task of safeguarding the Internet from relentless bugs. As the gameplay unfolds, the challenge intensifies with every passing second, creating an adrenaline-pumping experience. Players also have the opportunity to earn coveted medals that serve as a testament to their performance throughout the game.


Technical Implementations

  • Player Input: Implemented player input mechanisms, enabling seamless keyboard sharing in multiplayer mode, fostering collaboration and competition among players.

  • Animation Integration: Carefully integrated animations into the game's objects, ensuring that each element responds convincingly and appropriately within its respective state.

  • Enemy Behaviors: Designed and executed enemy behaviors, including their spawning patterns and movements. This included the creation of distinct enemy classes—one focused on targeting the Internet and another on pursuing the nearest player.

  • Life System: Developed a comprehensive life system, complete with behaviors for player and Internet lives, enhancing the game's depth and challenge.

  • SFX Editing and Implementation: Crafted and implemented immersive sound effects (SFX) to enrich the gaming experience, adding auditory dimension to the action.
  • Achievement System: Designed and integrated an achievement system, providing players with tangible rewards in the form of Internet Medals that reflect their performance in fulfilling their duty as defenders of the net.


Key Game Features

    Innovative shoot ‘em up:

    Internet Guardians injects fresh creativity into the shoot 'em up genre, with enemies emerging from all directions. Players must defend a target, offering a distinctive and innovative gaming experience.

    Local Multiplayer:

    Players can choose to embark on their heroic quest solo or team up with a friend, sharing the same keyboard for an exciting multiplayer adventure.

    Enemy Types:

    Bugs threaten the Internet, with some fixated on targeting the online realm and others relentlessly pursuing the players. Adaptability and strategy are key to victory.

    Achievement System:

    Players can earn Internet Medals, representing their accomplishments and prowess as guardians of the digital realm.
