About Game

This project serves as a comprehensive exploration of the capabilities achievable in VR development with Unity. The project immerses users in their own fantasy space within VR, offering the opportunity to interact with a variety of objects in unique and engaging ways.


Technical Implementation

  • Implementing interactive behaviors for each object, ensuring that each item responds uniquely to interactions:
    • A functional mirror that accurately reflects the environment and the player's actions.
    • A vintage camera with its own viewfinder capable of taking pictures and revealing them instantly.
    • Notebooks with hinged joints, featuring written messages within that can be narrated using text-to-voice.
    • Candles that can be extinguished by rapid movement or shaking, with a lighter to relight them.
    • A phone that plays videos
    • A TV remote that can turn the TV on and off, with the TV playing videos when turned on.
    • A vinyl record player that stops when the vinyl disk is grabbed and resumes when the disk is reinserted.
    • Wearable hats for user customization.
    • A watering can with audio feedback in various states.
    • A nerf gun that shoots nerf bullets at a determined velocity.

  • Teleportation for navigation across different areas within the VR room.

  • Objects adhering to sockets, allowing for extensive customization of the personal space.


Key Game Features

    Relaxing Atmosphere:

    The project aims to simulate a cozy personal space within a natural environment, creating a relaxing and immersive experience.

    Interactable Objects:

    Each object within the VR space offers a unique and engaging function, enhancing immersion and providing a satisfying tactile sensation.

    Re-organizable Objects:

    Objects are designed to fit into dedicated sockets, offering users full customization capabilities for their personal space.

    Immersive Sounds:

    The project utilizes 3D sound and other settings to enhance immersion, ensuring that objects produce realistic and spatial audio feedback.
