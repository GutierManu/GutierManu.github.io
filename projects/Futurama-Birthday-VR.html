About Game

This is a project created to celebrate the birthday of a friend, paying homage to their favorite show, Futurama, and their passion for Arduino. In this immersive VR experience, players embark on an exciting journey filled with puzzles and challenges, where they must shoot Bender clones and gather Arduino parts to construct a device that unveils a hidden birthday message at the end.


Technical Implementation

  • Imported 3D objects using the Sketchfab for Unity plugin, ensuring that the virtual environment faithfully represents the Futurama universe.

  • To capture the distinct visual style of the show, I meticulously edited and polished the materials of the imported 3D objects, implementing a toon shader that closely resembles the show's graphics.

  • Implemented the teleportation system, allowing players to navigate through the virtual world with ease.

  • Worked on interactive objects that respond realistically to player interaction, enhancing the immersion factor of the VR experience.

  • Designed the behavior of enemies, including the randomized loot players obtain when defeating them, as well as the dynamic difficulty scaling to provide replayable value.

  • Created the logic of the challenges, ensuring that their sequence and interactivity flow seamlessly to provide an immersive and engaging experience.

  • Implementation and editing of various visual effects (VFX), enhancing the overall aesthetics and immersion of the game.


Key Game Features

    Shooting Challenge:

    Take aim at Bender clones and collect valuable Arduino parts from your adversaries.

    Arduino Puzzle:

    Utilize the Arduino components acquired from defeating clones to craft a special device that plays a pivotal role in revealing the hidden birthday message.

    Interactable Objects:

    Interact with a variety of objects, each with its unique behaviors, offering that satisfying tactile sensation of handling objects in the immersive VR environment.

    Planet Express Exploration:

    Embark on a virtual exploration of the iconic headquarters of Planet Express, effortlessly navigating through the facility using teleportation.
