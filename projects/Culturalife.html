About Game

Culturalife emerged from a challenge with open-ended guidelines and a time limit of four days. The objective was to develop a small 2D simulation game prototype, akin to titles like The Sims and Stardew Valley, within the simulation game genre. The challenge demanded key elements, including a player character capable of interacting with the game world, a shop system for buying and selling items, and the ability to equip and unequip purchased items.


Technical Implementations

  • Character Customization System: Designed and implemented a robust character customization system, enabling players to personalize their avatars extensively. This system covers clothing, hairstyles, skin tone, body type, and even finer details such as name, surname, and pronouns. The scalable system allows for seamless asset replacement or addition.

  • Dynamic UI: Created a user interface that dynamically adjusts to the player's owned items, maintaining alignment and order while providing real-time feedback on the selected assets.

  • Art Assets: Created character art, icons, and customizable asset art, including animations, using Aseprite.

  • Shop System: Developed a shop system with a well-balanced economy, allowing players to purchase a finite quantity of items and sell them back at a reduced price.

  • Day/Night Cycle: Implemented a dynamic Day/Night cycle tied to the systemâ€™s clock. The ambient lighting evolves throughout the day, enhancing immersion.

  • Dialogue System: Designed a versatile dialogue system that extends to NPCs, signs, and the shop, enriching the player's interaction with the game world.

  • Input System: Developed a system which extends to keyboard and controller for user input.
  • Language Filter: Implemented a language filter in input fields to maintain a family-friendly and respectful environment.
  • Scene Navigation: Enabled seamless navigation between different scenes while maintaining consistent entrances and exits relevant to the player's location.
  • Level Design: Designed the game's levels and incorporated object behaviors to simulate depth, creating the illusion of objects in the foreground and background.


Key Game Features

    Full Character Customization:

    Players enjoy complete autonomy in customizing their avatars, from appearance to personal details.

    Day/Night Cycle:

    The immersive Day/Night cycle transitions from dusk to night and early morning, altering the ambiance and enhancing the gaming experience.

    Shopping Experience:

    The in-game shop offers a variety of items for purchase, with equipable hats and the option to sell items back to the shop at a reduced price.

    Cozy Atmosphere:

    Players explore and navigate a charming environment resembling a magical village nestled within the woods, providing a cozy and enchanting atmosphere.

    Dialogue Interactions:

    Engage with NPCs who offer interesting conversations and opportunities to explore the game world further.
