About App

Honduras Ancestral is an Augmented Reality (AR) application commissioned by CANATURH, the leading organization representing the private tourism sector in Honduras. This app offers users a unique and captivating way to explore the rich history and culture of Honduras, with a focus on two significant locations: the Ruins of Copan and the Fortress of San Fernando de Omoa.


Technical contributions

  • Augmented Reality Functionality: Implemented AR functionality based on image markers strategically placed at the Ruins of Copan and the Fortress of San Fernando de Omoa, creating an immersive and interactive experience.

  • Screenshot and Recording Modes: Introduced screenshot and recording modes, allowing users to capture exciting moments featuring AR characters and elements. These media files can be conveniently saved in a dedicated folder on the device and shared effortlessly via social media from the app.

  • Mini-Games: Developed mini-games within select AR scenes, including a 3D mini-game reminiscent of Angry Birds, where players must skillfully navigate a ball to score in one of the two rings representing the ancient Mesoamerican/Mayan Ballgame. Additionally, I created a mini-game where users assist the Mayan people by dragging corn within the AR scene and depositing it in a basket.

  • Photo and Video Player: Incorporated a photo and video player, enabling users to review and enjoy their latest captured media content.

  • UI Implementation: Developed and fine-tuned the user interface, ensuring smooth and intuitive navigation within the app.

  • Light Baking: Implemented light baking techniques to enhance and optimize the visual quality of the AR scenes.


Key App Features

    Augmented Reality:

    Immerse yourself in the Central American heritage with the assistance of image markers located at the Ruins of Copan and the Fortress of San Fernando de Omoa, bringing history to life in an interactive manner.

    Photo and Video Capture:

    Capture your most cherished moments with AR characters and elements, allowing you to share these captivating experiences with family and friends via social media.

    Mini-Games:

    Engage in mini-games integrated into select AR experiences, offering enjoyable and educational opportunities related to Mayan history.

    Historical Insights:

    Access detailed historical information about essential sites in Honduras, available in both Spanish and English.
