About Project

A VR experience created for the Central American Bank for Economic Integration (CABEI) and targeted exclusively for the Meta Quest 1. This unique VR adventure takes users on a rollercoaster ride within the CABEI building, featuring six distinct stations, each representing a CABEI-sponsored project in Central American countries. At each station, users encounter a detailed diorama and relevant project data. This project was developed as an advergame, designed to be showcased at CABEI-hosted events.


Technical Contribution

  • VR Camera and Integration: Carefully implemented the VR camera and ensured its smooth integration within the rollercoaster cart, guaranteeing an immersive and comfortable user experience throughout the ride.

  • Rollercoaster Track Creation: Designed and constructed the rollercoaster track to provide a seamless and thrilling ride. Utilized techniques to prevent motion sickness and dizziness, prioritizing user comfort.

  • Cart Controller: Developed a cart controller that ensures smooth stops at each station, allowing ample time for users to appreciate the dioramas and read project data. Additionally, I implemented logic to meet the designated time limit for the entire VR experience.
  • Building Blockout: Created a comprehensive blockout of the CABEI building's interior, ensuring that the VR environment aligns with the real-world structure.


Key Game Features

    Navigation:

    Embark on an exhilarating VR rollercoaster journey through the intricacies of the CABEI building, offering an engaging and immersive exploration of the bank's interior.

    Learning Experience:

    Encounter dioramas at each station, representing CABEI-sponsored projects in Central America. Users have the opportunity to delve into project details and gain valuable insights.
