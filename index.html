Culturalife

Culturalife emerged from a challenge with open-ended guidelines and a time limit of four days. The objective was to develop a small 2D simulation game prototype, akin to titles like The Sims and Stardew Valley, within the simulation game genre. The challenge demanded key elements, including a player character capable of interacting with the game world, a shop system for buying and selling items, and the ability to equip and unequip purchased items.

CABEI Rollercoaster

A VR experience created for the Central American Bank for Economic Integration (CABEI) and targeted exclusively for the Meta Quest 1. This unique VR adventure takes users on a rollercoaster ride within the CABEI building, featuring six distinct stations, each representing a CABEI-sponsored project in Central American countries. AR Portal

Personal

AR Portal

A project focused on demonstrating some of the capabilities of AR development in Unity. The app uses the camera to detect flat surfaces in the real world. Once these flat surfaces are detected, the user can instantiate a portal with 3 different environments (for this project, house areas are featured). The portal works both ways, the user is able to enter the digital environment by the front or the back side, and equally exit by any of these sides. The user is also able to exit (destroy) the environment by tapping on the pertinent button, allowing them to place the AR Portal in a different position.

Internet Guardians

Created for the Opera GX Game Jam held in Game Jolt in August 2021, this game was designed around the theme "UFOs and the Internet" and developed within a two-week time frame. In this game, players embark on a solo adventure or join forces in multiplayer mode, facing the formidable task of safeguarding the Internet from relentless bugs. As the gameplay unfolds, the challenge intensifies with every passing second, creating an adrenaline-pumping experience. Players also have the opportunity to earn coveted medals that serve as a testament to their performance throughout the game.
